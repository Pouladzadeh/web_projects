Parisa Pouladzadeh

Professor, School of Trades and Technology, Fleming College

I am a full-time professor in the School of Trades and Technology at Fleming College. My research lies in the fields of Machine Learning and Image Processing. Specific interests include:

  1. Mathematics for Machine Learning, Data Science, Practical Machine Learning
  2. Big data analysis and Social behavior prediction
  3. Cloud Computing
  4. Computer vision, Game development and Robotics
  5. Machine Learning and Image processing in Medical Applications
